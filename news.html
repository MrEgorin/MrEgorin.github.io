BioGENext Series 2

I had the privilege of attending the BioGENext Series 2 as a volunteer and listener, an event hosted by the National University of Kyiv-Mohyla Academy (NaUKMA). As a dedicated volunteer from the IMBG - Department of Molecular Oncology, I continue to support this important initiative. The highlight was Dr. Borys Olifirov's insightful presentation on Fluorescence Imaging: Theory and Applications. His shared resources and expertise deepened my understanding of fluorescence microscopy principles and their applications in modern scientific research.

I'm grateful to the organizers (YP Biotech, YURiA-PHARM LLC, Institute of Molecular Biology and Genetics, NAS of Ukraine, and others), Dr. Olifirov, my dedicated team, and National University of Kyiv-Mohyla Academy for creating such a valuable platform for learning and collaboration. This experience has further fueled my passion for advancing scientific knowledge and innovation.

Illumina Day 2025

Recently, I had the privilege of attending Illumina Day 2025, an incredible event for geneticists, molecular diagnostics specialists, and bioinformaticians. It was a fantastic opportunity to explore the latest advancements in genomics, including the unveiling of the new MiSeq system, which promises to revolutionize next-generation sequencing workflows.

Lectures by BioGENNex

I also attended a series of lectures by BioGENex, a wonderful initiative by YuriaPharm and YP Biotech. The lectures covered cutting-edge topics such as CRISPR/Cas technologies, rational drug design, proteomics, bioinformatics, wet molecular biology, recombinant technologies, and hybridoma technologies. As a bioinformatician with a biotechnological background from a technical university bachelor's program, I found this event incredibly inspiring and valuable for my professional growth.
