Illumina Day 2025

Recently, I had the privilege of attending Illumina Day 2025, an incredible event for geneticists, molecular diagnostics specialists, and bioinformaticians. It was a fantastic opportunity to explore the latest advancements in genomics, including the unveiling of the new MiSeq system, which promises to revolutionize next-generation sequencing workflows.

Lectures by BioGENNex

I also attended a series of lectures by BioGENNex, a wonderful initiative by YuriaPharm and YP Biotech. The lectures covered cutting-edge topics such as CRISPR/Cas technologies, rational drug design, proteomics, bioinformatics, wet molecular biology, recombinant technologies, and hybridoma technologies. As a bioinformatician with a biotechnological background from a technical university bachelor's program, I found this event incredibly inspiring and valuable for my professional growth.
