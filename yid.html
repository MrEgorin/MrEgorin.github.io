YID - About the Company

YID is a cutting-edge biotechnology company founded by Yehor Franchuk. Our mission is to revolutionize the field of biotechnology through the integration of artificial intelligence and computational biology.

Our Mission

At YID, we aim to push the boundaries of what's possible in biotechnology. By leveraging AI, genomics, drug discovery, and protein engineering, we strive to create innovative solutions for personalized medicine and beyond.

Key Achievements

  • CRISPR-Cas9 Technologies: Development of precision genome editing tools.
  • Drug Design: Implementation of machine learning algorithms for drug discovery.
  • Multi-Omics Analysis: Providing personalized medicine solutions through advanced data analysis.

Team Leadership

As the founder and CEO, I lead a multidisciplinary team of experts in AI, bioinformatics, and molecular biology. Together, we are driving innovation and making a difference in the world of biotechnology.
